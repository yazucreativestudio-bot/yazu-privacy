Yazu Creative Studio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Yazu Creative Studio and its application "Yazu Pin Automation" ("the App", "we", "our") handle information in connection with use of the Pinterest API.

About this application

Yazu Pin Automation is a private, personal-use tool operated by Yazu Creative Studio, the owner of the connected Pinterest business account. It is used to automatically create Pins on the operator's own Pinterest business account by retrieving product information (titles, descriptions, images, and links) from the operator's own Etsy shop. The App is not offered to the public and does not serve other users.

Information we access

When authorized through Pinterest OAuth, the App accesses the following from the operator's own Pinterest account:

  • Basic account information needed to identify the connected account
  • A list of the account's boards (to select where Pins are published)
  • Permission to create Pins on the connected account

The App also reads publicly available product data from the operator's own Etsy shop in order to compose Pins.

How we use information

Accessed information is used solely to:

  • Create and publish Pins to the operator's own Pinterest boards
  • Match each Pin to the correct board

We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing the operator's own content.

Data storage and retention

The App stores only the OAuth access and refresh tokens required to maintain the connection to the operator's own Pinterest account. These tokens are stored securely as encrypted secrets in the operator's own development environment and are never shared. No Pinterest user data is collected from, or stored about, any third party.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any accessed information with third parties. The App communicates only with the official Pinterest API and the operator's own Etsy shop.

Third-party services

The App relies on:

  • The Pinterest API, governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Developer Terms
  • The Etsy API, governed by Etsy's own Privacy Policy

Data deletion

Because the App stores no third-party data, there is no third-party data to delete. The operator may revoke the App's access at any time from their Pinterest account settings, which immediately ends all access.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly process data from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
